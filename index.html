Happy Birthday, Zoe

Another year for you, and somehow you just keep getting more you, which is exactly what I'd wish for. You're proof that the good stuff really does come in small packages, and I mean that with full love and only a little teasing.

You've always had this eye for color, and green just makes sense on you. It's calm and a bit wild at the same time, kind of like your personality. And of course emerald is your stone. Rare, catches the light in a way nothing else does, quietly one of the most valuable things in any room. Sound familiar?

Then there's the art. Hand you a pencil for five minutes and you'll have sketched something that makes the rest of us wonder why we ever stopped doodling in our notebook margins. You notice things the rest of us walk past, and it shows in everything you draw.

You're also one of the kindest people I know, honestly almost annoyingly so. Sharp with the jokes, soft with the heart, and terrible at hiding how much you actually care about people even when you try to play it cool.

And can we talk about the stickers. The sheer amount of unnecessary, deeply specific, borderline unhinged stickers you own is basically its own personality trait. A frog in a tiny top hat, a strawberry with human teeth, whatever it is, you'll find it, you'll love it, and it'll end up somewhere completely unrelated because it made you laugh.

You also barely leave the house, which honestly I respect, because the outside world hasn't earned you yet.

Happy birthday, Zoe. Short in height, immeasurable in everything that actually matters. Almost everything. There's one thing we still need to measure, so keep scrolling.
